A couple quick notes:

  • This is pre-alpha software, bugs will occur. Please use the bug tracker to report any bugs you find. Every attempt will be made to fix them promptly.
  • Multiserver/J2EE installs have limited support. Please see index.cfm for more information.
  • I use this myself every day, and it's very, very useful, at least to me. That's why I'm sharing it. I hope it proves as useful to you and makes your ColdFusion development experience that much more enjoyable.

Thanks for downloading and checking out TypeCast and please feel free to provide any feedback via the forums, bug tracker, or wiki (all of which are on RIAForge.com).
